NINJA HATTORI
Cartoon Show

Hattori is a strict, by-the-book ninja, and exudes good leadership. He can be extreme, and has is standards that he expects everyone to reach, but even if grudgingly, he helps Kenichi with his troubles, and despite his arguments with his brother Shinzo, he still loves him and always encourages him to improve. Despite his flaws, he is a genuine good egg and cares deeply for his family.
